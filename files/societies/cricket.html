Where and when does your society meet?

We meet once per week at Fenners for nets during the winter, and then we have weekly fixtures during Easter term, as well as outdoor training.

Does your team play in a league or Cuppers?

Yes, we play in Cuppers. We are a fairly strong side, and have reached the semi-finals in each of the last two years.

What roles are there?

A new captain is decided upon each year, alongside the normal roles within a cricket team.

What ability does someone need to join?

College abilities are very mixed, and most abilities are welcome. It is recommended that you have played a bit before, but you don't need to have played to a high standard at all.

What equipment is provided? What personal kit does someone need?

We provide cricket balls, stumps etc, but you will either need to bring your own personal equipment or share with others (some do).

Are there any costs?

There are no financial contributions required from individuals.
