When and where does your society/team meet?

We have training sessions every Saturday from 6-8pm in the University Sports Centre. This might change next year, but will be announced on the clubs facebook page (https://www.facebook.com/CUHandball/)

What do you do?

Handball, of course. A fast and physical ball sport known all across Europe except for the UK.

Does your team play in a league or cuppers?

We have multiple friendly matches during the year, participate in the UK University Championships, and play Varsity against Oxford. There is an option for advanced players to play in a UK league as well.

What roles are there?

There are five different positions you can play: A strong pivot, tall back-shooters, tactical centre player, technically skilled wingers, and a fearless goalkeeper.

What ability does someone need to join?

Two hands is a good start, otherwise we will surely find a suitable position for you. Even if you have never played before.

What equipment is provided? What personal kit does someone need?

Regular sports clothes are necessary (including indoor sports shoes). Everything else is provided by the club.

Are there any costs?

Cost are termwise: 20£ for a single term, 35£ for two terms, and 50£ for the whole year. This is pretty much spent completely for booking the hall.
