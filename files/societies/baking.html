When and where does your society/team meet?

We meet every Sunday at 4 in J8.

What do you do?

Every week we choose a baking project and make it together, with varying degrees of success!

What ability does someone need to join?

No baking knowledge needed, if you want to come and eat things and tell us how good they were that's fine too.

What equipment is provided? What personal kit does someone need?

No equipment necessary.

Are there any costs?

Small contributions to ingredients costs are asked for, almost always less than one pound.
